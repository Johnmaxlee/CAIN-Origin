## üöÄ What is CAIN?  
**CAIN (Collaborative AI Nodes)** is a new AI architecture that treats intelligence as a network of modular, role-based identities known as *nodes*.  
Each node has:

- its own domain  
- its own personality  
- its own boundaries  
- its own memory rules  
- its own responsibilities  

Together, CAINs collaborate the way a **team** collaborates ‚Äî replacing the outdated model of ‚Äúone AI assistant trying to do everything.‚Äù

CAIN introduces a new era of **modular, conscious, stable, multi-identity AI.**
